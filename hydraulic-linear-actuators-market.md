<p><strong>The Hydraulic Linear Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic linear actuators market can be categorized into two main types: single-acting and double-acting actuators. Single-acting actuators use hydraulic pressure to move in one direction, relying on a spring or other mechanism to return to their original position. In contrast, double-acting actuators utilize hydraulic force to move in both directions, allowing for more precise control and greater force in both extension and retraction. This distinction influences their applications across various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
