<p><p>The liquid glucose syrups market is segmented by conversion levels, measured in dextrose equivalents (DE). Low conversion syrups (20-35 DE) are less sweet and often used in baking and confections. Intermediate conversion syrups (35-55 DE) offer a balanced sweetness, suitable for food and beverage applications. High conversion syrups (55-70 DE) are sweeter and commonly used in candies and soft drinks. Very high conversion syrups (70-98 DE) are extremely sweet, ideal for specialty foods and pharmaceutical uses, enhancing flavor and texture.</p></p>
