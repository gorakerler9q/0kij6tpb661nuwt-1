<p><p>The dried skimmed milk powder (SMP) market is categorized into three types based on the heat treatment during processing: Low Heat SMP, Medium Heat SMP, and High Heat SMP. Low Heat SMP is produced at lower temperatures and retains more functional properties, making it ideal for reconstitution. Medium Heat SMP undergoes moderate heat treatment, enhancing shelf life while preserving some functionality. High Heat SMP is subjected to high temperatures, resulting in a longer shelf life and suitability for baking and confectionery applications, albeit with reduced solubility.</p></p>
