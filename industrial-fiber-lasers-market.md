<p><strong>The Industrial Fiber Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fiber lasers market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers emit a steady beam of light, ideal for applications requiring constant energy output, such as cutting and welding metals. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers generate short, intense bursts of energy, making them suitable for precise tasks like marking, engraving, and micromachining. Both types serve various industries, driving advancements in manufacturing, telecommunications, and medical technologies.</p></p>
