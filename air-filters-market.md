<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Air Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Filters</li><li>Sub-HEPA Filters</li><li>Secondary Filters</li><li>HEPA and ULPA Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air filters market comprises various types designed to enhance air quality. Pre-filters capture large particles and extend the life of primary filters. Sub-HEPA filters provide medium filtration, removing smaller particles than pre-filters. Secondary filters act as an additional layer, improving overall filtration efficiency. HEPA filters are renowned for trapping 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns, while ULPA filters offer even higher efficiency, targeting 99.999% of ultra-fine particles. Each type serves specific applications in residential and industrial settings.</p></p>
