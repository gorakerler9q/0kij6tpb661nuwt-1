<p><p>The water purifier market encompasses various technologies, each catering to different purification needs. Activated carbon purifiers effectively remove impurities and odors. UV technology utilizes ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses, ensuring safe drinking water. Chemical-based purifiers often use chlorine or iodine for disinfection. Reverse osmosis systems employ a membrane to filter out contaminants and dissolved solids, providing clean water. Other emerging technologies, such as magnetic and ceramic filters, also contribute to the diverse water purification landscape, enhancing accessibility and safety.</p></p>
