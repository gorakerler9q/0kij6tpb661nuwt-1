<p><ul><li>DC Electric Actuator</li><li>AC Electric Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric actuator market primarily comprises two types: DC electric actuators and AC electric actuators. DC electric actuators utilize direct current for operation, offering advantages like high torque at low speeds and precise control, making them suitable for applications in robotics and aerospace. In contrast, AC electric actuators operate on alternating current, providing greater efficiency for high-power applications such as industrial automation and HVAC systems. Both types cater to the growing demand for automation across various sectors, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
