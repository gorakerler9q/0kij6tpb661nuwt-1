<p><p>The butter fat fraction market encompasses various components derived from milk fat. MFGM, or milk fat globule membrane, plays a crucial role in emulsifying and stabilizing fat globules, enhancing nutritional benefits. Phospholipids, found in MFGM, are vital for cell membrane structure and function, contributing to cardiovascular health. Gangliosides, another component, are involved in brain development and function, making them significant in infant nutrition. Together, these fractions offer diverse applications in food, dairy, and health products, catering to evolving consumer demands.</p></p>
